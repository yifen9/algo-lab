### **Problem Statement**

<p>
Given is a sequence of $N$integers $A_1,A_2,\ldots,A_N$. Print the number, modulo $998244353$, of sequences of $N$integers $X_1,X_2,\ldots,X_N$satisfying all of the following conditions:
</p>

<ul>

<li>
$1 \leq X_i \leq A_i$
</li>

<li>
$X_i \neq X_{i+1} (1 \leq i \leq N-1)$
</li>

</ul>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Constraints**

<ul>

<li>
$2 \leq N \leq 5 \times 10^5$
</li>

<li>
$1 \leq A_i \leq 10^9$
</li>

</ul>

</section>

</div>

---

<div>

<div>

<section>

### **Input**

<p>
Input is given from Standard Input in the following format:
</p>

<div>

$N$$A_1$$A_2$$\ldots$$A_N$
</div>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Output**

<p>
Print the answer.
</p>

</section>

</div>

</div>

---

<section>
