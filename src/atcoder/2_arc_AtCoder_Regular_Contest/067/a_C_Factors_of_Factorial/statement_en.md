### **Problem Statement**

<p>
You are given an integer $N$.
Find the number of the positive divisors of $N!$, modulo $10^9+7$.
</p>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Constraints**

<ul>

<li>
$1≤N≤10^3$
</li>

</ul>

</section>

</div>

---

<div>

<div>

<section>

### **Input**

<p>
The input is given from Standard Input in the following format:
</p>

<div>

$N$
</div>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Output**

<p>
Print the number of the positive divisors of $N!$, modulo $10^9+7$.
</p>

</section>

</div>

</div>

---

<div>

<section>

### **Sample Input 1**

<div>

3

</div>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Sample Output 1**

<div>

4

</div>

<p>

</p>
