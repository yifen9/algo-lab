<section>

### **Problem Statement**

<p>
Given is a positive integer $N$.
Find the value $\displaystyle\sum_{i=1}^N \left[ \frac{N}{i} \right]$.
</p>

<p>
Here, for a real number $x$, $[x]$denotes the largest integer not exceeding $x$.
</p>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Constraints**

<ul>

<li>
$1 \leq N \leq 10^{12}$
</li>

<li>
$N$is an integer.
</li>

</ul>

</section>

</div>

---

<div>

<div>

<section>

### **Input**

<p>
Input is given from Standard Input in the following format:
</p>

<div>

$N$
</div>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Output**

<p>
Print the answer.
</p>

</section>

</div>

</div>

---

<div>

<section>

### **Sample Input 1**

<div>

3

</div>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Sample Output 1**

<div>

5

</div>

<p>
We have $\left[ \frac{3}{1} \right]+\left[ \frac{3}{2} \right]+\left[ \frac{3}{3} \right]=3+1+1=5$.
</p>

</section>

</div>

10000000000

<div>

### **Sample Output 2**

<div>

231802823220

</div>

</p>

</section>
