<div>

<section>

### **Problem Statement**

<p>
Print how many distinct integers there are in given five integers $A, B, C, D$, and $E$.
</p>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Constraints**

<ul>

<li>
$0 \leq A, B, C, D, E \leq 100$
</li>

<li>
All values in input are integers.
</li>

</ul>

</section>

</div>

---

<div>

<div>

<section>

### **Input**

<p>
Input is given from Standard Input in the following format:
</p>

<div>

$A$$B$$C$$D$$E$
</div>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Output**

<p>
Print the answer.
</p>

</section>

</div>

</div>

---

<div>

<section>

### **Sample Input 1**

<div>

31 9 24 31 24

</div>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Sample Output 1**

<div>

3

</div>

<p>
In the given five integers $31, 9, 24, 31$, and $24$, there are three distinct integers $9, 24$, and $31$.
Thus, $3$should be printed.
</p>

</section>

</div>

</div>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Sample Output 2**

<div>

1

</div>

</section>
