### **Problem Statement**

<p>
You will turn on the air conditioner if, and only if, the temperature of the room is $30$degrees Celsius or above.
</p>

<p>
The current temperature of the room is $X$degrees Celsius. Will you turn on the air conditioner?
</p>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Constraints**

<ul>

<li>
$-40 \leq X \leq 40$
</li>

<li>
$X$is an integer.
</li>

</ul>

</section>

</div>

---

<div>

<div>

<section>

### **Input**

<p>
Input is given from Standard Input in the following format:
</p>

<div>

$X$
</div>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Output**

<p>
Print `Yes`if you will turn on the air conditioner; print `No`otherwise.
</p>

</section>

</div>

</div>

---

<div>

<section>

### **Sample Input 1**

<div>

25

</div>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Sample Output 1**

<div>

No

</div>

</section>

</div>

---

<div>

<section>

### **Sample Input 2**

<div>

30

</div>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Sample Output 2**

<div>

Yes

</div>

</section>
