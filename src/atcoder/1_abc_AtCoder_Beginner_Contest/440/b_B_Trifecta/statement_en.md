<p>
Score : $200$points
</p>

<div>

<section>

### **Problem Statement**

<p>
$N$horses numbered $1$to $N$had a race.
</p>

<p>
All horses started simultaneously, and horse $i$took $T_i$seconds from the start to the goal.
</p>

<p>
Find the numbers of the horses that finished in $1$st, $2$nd, and $3$rd places. It is guaranteed that all $T_i$are distinct.
</p>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Constraints**

<ul>

<li>
$3\leq N \leq 32$
</li>

<li>
$1\leq T_i \leq 200$
</li>

<li>
All $T_i$are distinct.
</li>

<li>
All input values are integers.
</li>

</ul>

</section>

</div>

---

<div>

<div>

<section>

### **Input**

<p>
The input is given from Standard Input in the following format:
</p>

<div>

$N$$T_1$$\dots$$T_N$
</div>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Output**

<p>
Output the numbers of the horses that finished in $1$st, $2$nd, and $3$rd places, in this order, separated by spaces.
</p>

</section>

</div>

</div>

---

<div>

<section>

### **Sample Input 1**

<div>

4
100 110 105 95

</div>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Sample Output 1**

<div>

4 1 3

</div>

<p>
The horses finished in the order $4, 1, 3, 2$. Output the numbers for $1$st, $2$nd, and $3$rd places, which are $4, 1, 3$, in this order, separated by spaces.
</p>

</section>

</div>

---

<div>

<section>

### **Sample Input 2**

<div>

8
72 74 69 70 73 75 71 77

</div>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Sample Output 2**

<div>

3 4 7

</div>

</section>
