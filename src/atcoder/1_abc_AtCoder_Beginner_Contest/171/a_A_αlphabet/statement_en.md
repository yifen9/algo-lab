### **Problem Statement**

<p>
An uppercase or lowercase English letter $\alpha$will be given as input.
If $\alpha$is uppercase, print `A`; if it is lowercase, print `a`.
</p>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Constraints**

<ul>

<li>
$\alpha$is an uppercase (`A`- `Z`) or lowercase (`a`- `z`) English letter.
</li>

</ul>

</section>

</div>
