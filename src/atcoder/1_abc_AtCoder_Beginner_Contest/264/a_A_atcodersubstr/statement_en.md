### **Problem Statement**

<p>
Print the $L$-th through $R$-th characters of the string `atcoder`.
</p>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Constraints**

<ul>

<li>
$L$and $R$are integers.
</li>

<li>
$1 \le L \le R \le 7$
</li>

</ul>

</section>

</div>

---

<div>

<div>

<section>

<p>
Input is given from Standard Input in the following format:
</p>

<div>

$L$$R$
</div>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Output**

<p>
Print the answer.
</p>

</section>

</div>

</div>
