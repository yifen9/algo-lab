<section>

### **Problem Statement**

<p>
The 
<strong>
digit sum
</strong>
of a positive integer $n$is defined as the sum of the digits when $n$is represented in decimal. For example, the digit sum of $2026$is $2+0+2+6=10$.
</p>

<p>
Find the number of positive integers not exceeding $N$whose digit sum is $K$.
</p>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Constraints**

<ul>

<li>
$1\leq N,K \leq 10^5$
</li>

<li>
All input values are integers.
</li>

</ul>

</section>

</div>

---

<div>

<div>

<section>

### **Input**

<p>
The input is given from Standard Input in the following format:
</p>

<div>

$N$$K$
</div>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Output**

<p>
Output the answer.
</p>

</section>

</div>

</div>

---

<div>

<section>

### **Sample Input 1**

<div>

30 4

</div>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Sample Output 1**

<div>

3

</div>

<p>
Among the positive integers not exceeding $30$, there are three integers whose digit sum is $4$: $4,13,22$.
</p>

</section>

</div>
