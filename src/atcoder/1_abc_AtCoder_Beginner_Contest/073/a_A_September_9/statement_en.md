### **Problem Statement**

<p>
It is September $9$in Japan now.
</p>

<p>
You are given a two-digit integer $N$. Answer the question: Is $9$contained in the decimal notation of $N$?
</p>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Constraints**

<ul>

<li>
$10≤N≤99$
</li>

</ul>

</section>

</div>

---

<div>

<div>

<section>

### **Input**

<p>
Input is given from Standard Input in the following format:
</p>

<div>

$N$
</div>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Output**

<p>
If $9$is contained in the decimal notation of $N$, print `Yes`; if not, print `No`.
</p>

</section>

</div>

</div>

---

<div>

<section>

### **Sample Input 1**

<div>

29

</div>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Sample Output 1**

<div>

Yes

</div>

<p>
The one's digit of $29$is $9$.
</p>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Sample Input 2**

72

</div>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Sample Output 2**

No

</div>

<p>
$72$does not contain $9$.
</p>

</section>

91
