<div>

<section>

### **Problem Statement**

<p>
There is an $N$-car train.
</p>

<p>
You are given an integer $i$. Find the value of $j$such that the following statement is true: "the $i$-th car from the front of the train is the $j$-th car from the back."
</p>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Constraints**

<ul>

<li>
$1 \leq N \leq 100$
</li>

<li>
$1 \leq i \leq N$
</li>

</ul>

</section>

</div>

---

<div>

<div>

<section>

### **Input**

<p>
Input is given from Standard Input in the following format:
</p>

<div>

$N$$i$
</div>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Output**

<p>
Print the answer.
</p>

</section>

</div>

</div>

---

<div>

<section>

### **Sample Input 1**

<div>

4 2

</div>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Sample Output 1**

<div>

3

</div>

<p>
The second car from the front of a $4$-car train is the third car from the back.
</p>

</section>

</div>

---

<div>

<section>

### **Sample Input 2**

<div>

1 1

</div>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Sample Output 2**

<div>

1

</div>

</section>

</div>

---

<div>

<section>

### **Sample Input 3**

<div>

15 11

</div>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Sample Output 3**

<div>

5

</div>

</section>
