<section>

### **Problem Statement**

<p>
Takahashi is wearing white now.
</p>

<p>
He wears black on the day after he wears white, and he wears white on the day after he wears black.
</p>

<p>
What color will he wear $N$days later?
</p>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Constraints**

<ul>

<li>
$N$is an integer.
</li>

<li>
$1 \leq N \leq 30$
</li>

</ul>

</section>

</div>

---

<div>

<div>

<section>

### **Input**

<p>
Input is given from Standard Input in the following format:
</p>

<div>

$N$
</div>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Output**

<p>
If he will wear white $N$days later, print `White`; if he will wear black, print `Black`.
</p>

</section>

</div>

</div>

---

<div>

<section>

### **Sample Input 1**

<div>

2

</div>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Sample Output 1**

<div>

White

</div>

<p>
Takahashi will wear black one day later and wear white two days later.
</p>

</section>

</div>

---

<div>

<section>

### **Sample Input 2**

<div>

5

</div>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Sample Output 2**

<div>

Black

</div>

</section>

</div>
