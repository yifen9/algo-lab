<section>

### **Problem Statement**

<p>
You drew lottery $N$times. In the $i$-th draw, you got an item of the kind represented by a string $S_i$.
</p>

<p>
How many kinds of items did you get?
</p>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Constraints**

<ul>

<li>
$1 \leq N \leq 2\times 10^5$
</li>

<li>
$S_i$consists of lowercase English letters and has a length between $1$and $10$(inclusive).
</li>

</ul>

</section>

</div>

---

<div>

<div>

<section>

### **Input**

<p>
Input is given from Standard Input in the following format:
</p>

<div>

$N$$S_1$$:$$S_N$
</div>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Output**

<p>
Print the number of kinds of items you got.
</p>

</section>

</div>

</div>

---

<div>

<section>

### **Sample Input 1**

<div>

3
apple
orange
apple

</div>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Sample Output 1**

<div>

2

</div>

<p>
You got two kinds of items: `apple`and `orange`.
</p>

</section>

</div>

<div>

grape

</div>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Sample Output 2**

<div>

1

</div>

</section>

</div>

4

</div>
