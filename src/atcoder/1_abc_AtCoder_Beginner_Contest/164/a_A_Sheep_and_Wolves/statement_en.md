<div>

<section>

### **Problem Statement**

<p>
There are $S$sheep and $W$wolves.
</p>

<p>
If the number of wolves is greater than or equal to that of sheep, the wolves will attack the sheep.
</p>

<p>
If the wolves will attack the sheep, print `unsafe`; otherwise, print `safe`.
</p>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Constraints**

<ul>

<li>
$1 \leq S \leq 100$
</li>

<li>
$1 \leq W \leq 100$
</li>

</ul>

</section>

</div>

---

<div>

<div>

<section>

### **Input**

<p>
Input is given from Standard Input in the following format:
</p>

<div>

$S$$W$
</div>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Output**

<p>
If the wolves will attack the sheep, print `unsafe`; otherwise, print `safe`.
</p>

</section>

</div>

</div>

---

<div>

<section>

### **Sample Input 1**

<div>

4 5

</div>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Sample Output 1**

<div>

unsafe

</div>

<p>
There are four sheep and five wolves. The number of wolves is not less than that of sheep, so they will attack them.
</p>

</section>

</div>

---

<div>

<section>

### **Sample Input 2**

<div>

100 2

</div>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Sample Output 2**

<div>

safe

</div>

<p>
Many a sheep drive away two wolves.
</p>

</section>

</div>

<div>

10 10

</div>

</section>

<div>

<section>

unsafe

</div>

</section>
