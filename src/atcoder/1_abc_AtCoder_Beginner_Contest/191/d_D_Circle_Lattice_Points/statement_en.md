### **Problem Statement**

<p>
We have a circle of radius $R$centered at $(X, Y)$.

Find the number of grid points (points whose $x$- and $y$-coordinates are both integers) within or on the circle.
</p>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Constraints**

<ul>

<li>
$|X| \le 10^5$
</li>

<li>
$|Y| \le 10^5$
</li>

<li>
$0 \lt R \le 10^5$
</li>

<li>
Each of $X$, $Y$, and $R$has at most four digits after the decimal point.
</li>

</ul>

</section>

</div>

---

<div>

<div>

<section>

### **Input**

<p>
Input is given from Standard Input in the following format:
</p>

<div>

$X$$Y$$R$
</div>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Output**

<p>
Print the answer.
</p>

</section>

</div>

</div>

---

<div>

<section>

### **Sample Input 1**

<div>

0.2 0.8 1.1

</div>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Sample Output 1**

<div>

3

</div>
