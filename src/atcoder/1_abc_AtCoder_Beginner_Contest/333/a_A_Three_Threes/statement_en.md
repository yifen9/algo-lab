### **Problem Statement**

<p>
You are given an integer $N$between $1$and $9$, inclusive, as input.
</p>

<p>
Concatenate $N$copies of the digit $N$and print the resulting string.
</p>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Constraints**

<ul>

<li>
$N$is an integer between $1$and $9$, inclusive.
</li>

</ul>

</section>

</div>

---

<div>

<div>

<section>

### **Input**

<p>
The input is given from Standard Input in the following format:
</p>

<div>

$N$
</div>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Output**

<p>
Print the answer.
</p>

</section>

</div>

</div>

---

<div>

<section>

### **Sample Input 1**

<div>

3

</div>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Sample Output 1**

<div>

333

</div>

<p>
Concatenate three copies of the digit $3$to yield the string `333`.
</p>

</section>
