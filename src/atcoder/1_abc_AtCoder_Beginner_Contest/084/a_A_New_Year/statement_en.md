<section>

### **Problem Statement**

<p>
How many hours do we have until New Year at $M$o'clock (24-hour notation) on $30$th, December?
</p>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Constraints**

<ul>

<li>
$1≤M≤23$
</li>

<li>
$M$is an integer.
</li>

</ul>

</section>

</div>

---

<div>

<div>

<section>

### **Input**

<p>
Input is given from Standard Input in the following format:
</p>

<div>

$M$
</div>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Output**

<p>
If we have $x$hours until New Year at $M$o'clock on $30$th, December, print $x$.
</p>

</section>

</div>

</div>

---

<div>

<section>

### **Sample Input 1**

<div>

21

</div>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Sample Output 1**

<div>

27

</div>

<p>
We have $27$hours until New Year at $21$o'clock on $30$th, December.
</p>

</section>

</div>

---

<div>

<section>

### **Sample Input 2**

<div>

12

</div>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Sample Output 2**

<div>

36

</div>

</section>
