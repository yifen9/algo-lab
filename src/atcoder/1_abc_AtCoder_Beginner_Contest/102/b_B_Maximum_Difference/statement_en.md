### **Problem Statement**

<p>
You are given an integer sequence $A$of length $N$.
Find the maximum absolute difference of two elements (with different indices) in $A$.
</p>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Constraints**

<ul>

<li>
$2 \leq N \leq 100$
</li>

<li>
$1 \leq A_i \leq 10^9$
</li>

<li>
All values in input are integers.
</li>

</ul>

</section>

</div>

---

<div>

<div>

<section>

### **Input**

<p>
Input is given from Standard Input in the following format:
</p>

<div>

$N$$A_1$$A_2$$...$$A_N$
</div>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Output**

<p>
Print the maximum absolute difference of two elements (with different indices) in $A$.
</p>

</section>

</div>

</div>

---

<div>

<section>

### **Sample Input 1**

<div>

4
1 4 6 3

</div>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Sample Output 1**

<div>

5

</div>

<p>
The maximum absolute difference of two elements is $A_3-A_1=6-1=5$.
</p>

</section>

</div>
