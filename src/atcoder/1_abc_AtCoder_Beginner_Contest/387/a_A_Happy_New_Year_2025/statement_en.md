### **Problem Statement**

<p>
You are given two positive integers $A$and $B$.
</p>

<p>
Output the square of $A + B$.
</p>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Constraints**

<ul>

<li>
$1 \leq A,B \leq 2025$
</li>

<li>
All input values are integers.
</li>

</ul>

</section>

</div>

---

<div>

<div>

<section>

### **Input**

<p>
The input is given from Standard Input in the following format:
</p>

<div>

$A$$B$
</div>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Output**

<p>
Print the answer.
</p>

</section>

</div>

</div>
