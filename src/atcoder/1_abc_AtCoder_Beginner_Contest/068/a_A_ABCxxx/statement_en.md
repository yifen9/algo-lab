<section>

### **Problem Statement**

<p>
This contest, 
<em>
AtCoder Beginner Contest
</em>
, is abbreviated as 
<em>
ABC
</em>
.
</p>

<p>
When we refer to a specific round of ABC, a three-digit number is appended after ABC. For example, ABC680 is the 680th round of ABC.
</p>

<p>
What is the abbreviation for the $N$-th round of ABC? Write a program to output the answer.
</p>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Constraints**

<ul>

<li>
$100 ≤ N ≤ 999$
</li>

</ul>

</section>

</div>

---

<div>

<div>

<section>

### **Input**

<p>
Input is given from Standard Input in the following format:
</p>

<div>

$N$
</div>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Output**

<p>
Print the abbreviation for the $N$-th round of ABC.
</p>

</section>

</div>

</div>

---

<div>

<section>

### **Sample Input 1**

<div>

100

</div>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Sample Output 1**

<div>

ABC100

</div>

<p>
The 100th round of ABC is ABC100.
</p>

</section>

</div>

999

</div>

<div>
