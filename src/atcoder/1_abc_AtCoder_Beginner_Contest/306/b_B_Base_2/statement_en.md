### **Problem Statement**

<p>
You are given a sequence $A=(A_0,A_1,\dots,A_{63})$of length $64$consisting of $0$and $1$.
</p>

<p>
Find $A_0 2^0 + A_1 2^1 + \dots + A_{63} 2^{63}$.
</p>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Constraints**

<ul>

<li>
$A_i$is $0$or $1$.
</li>

</ul>

</section>

</div>

---

<div>

<div>

<section>

<p>
The input is given from Standard Input in the following format:
</p>

<div>

$A_0$$A_1$$\dots$$A_{63}$
</div>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

</p>

</section>
