<p>
Score : $100$points
</p>

<div>

<section>

### **Problem Statement**

<p>
$1$foot is $12$inches.
</p>

<p>
How many inches is $A$feet $B$inches?
</p>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Constraints**

<ul>

<li>
$1 \leq A \leq 8$
</li>

<li>
$0 \leq B \leq 11$
</li>

<li>
All input values are integers.
</li>

</ul>

</section>

</div>

---

<div>

<div>

<section>

### **Input**

<p>
The input is given from Standard Input in the following format:
</p>

<div>

$A$$B$
</div>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Output**

<p>
Output the answer in one line. Omit the unit (inches).
</p>

</section>

</div>

</div>

---

<div>

<section>

### **Sample Input 1**

<div>

6 7

</div>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Sample Output 1**

<div>

79

</div>

<p>
$6$feet $7$inches is $6 \times 12 + 7 = 79$inches.
</p>

</section>

</div>

---

<div>

<section>

### **Sample Input 2**

<div>

4 11

</div>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Sample Output 2**

<div>

59

</div>

<p>
$4$feet $11$inches is $4 \times 12 + 11 = 59$inches.
</p>

</section>

</div>

---

<div>

<section>

### **Sample Input 3**

<div>

8 0

</div>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Sample Output 3**

<div>

96

</div>

<p>
$8$feet $0$inches is $8 \times 12 + 0 = 96$inches.
</p>

</section>

</div>
