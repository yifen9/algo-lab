### **Problem Statement**

<p>
You are given a positive integer $N$.
</p>

<p>
Output $N, N-1, \dots, 1$in this order, separated by commas `,`.
</p>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Constraints**

<ul>

<li>
$1 \leq N \leq 9$
</li>

<li>
$N$is an integer.
</li>

</ul>

</section>

</div>

---

<div>

<div>

<section>

### **Input**

<p>
The input is given from Standard Input in the following format:
</p>

<div>

$N$
</div>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Output**

<p>
Output $N, N-1, \dots, 1$in this order, separated by commas.
</p>

</section>
