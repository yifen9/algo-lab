<div>

<section>

### **Problem Statement**

<p>
You are given two positive integers $A$and $B$. Compare the magnitudes of these numbers.
</p>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Constraints**

<ul>

<li>
$1 ≤ A, B ≤ 10^{100}$
</li>

<li>
Neither $A$nor $B$begins with a `0`.
</li>

</ul>

</section>

</div>

---

<div>

<div>

<section>

### **Input**

<p>
Input is given from Standard Input in the following format:
</p>

<div>

$A$$B$
</div>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Output**

<p>
Print `GREATER`if $A>B$, `LESS`if $A<B$and `EQUAL`if $A=B$.
</p>

</section>

</div>

</div>

---

<div>

<section>

### **Sample Input 1**

<div>

36
24

</div>

</section>

</div>

<div>

<section>

### **Sample Output 1**

<div>

GREATER

</div>

<p>
Since $36>24$, print `GREATER`.
</p>

</section>

</div>
